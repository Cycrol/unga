A Simple, Informative Guide to a Cannibal's Diet

Complete Human Body Noodles DIFFICULT FOR NEW COOKS! NOT RECOMMENDED FOR KITCHEN WEAKLINGS!

  1. Strangle a fellow human, preferably one of a healthy age! Stature is not really important for this dish!
  2. Create a distal incision into the peritoneal cavity. Extract all innards, including small intestines, large intestines, livers, pancreas, etc. NYLON GLOVES ARE RECOMMENDED FOR THIS STAGE IF YOU WISH FOR A CLEAN EVISCERATION OF VISCERAL CONTENTS FROM THE PERITONEAL CAVITY!
  3. Place a tube into the femoral artery of victim's corpse, draining them of their blood.
  4. Add agar into blood, and cool for 24 hours to make jelly blood noodles. Set these aside for a while.
  5. Place innards into water to wash off blood and excess waste material.
  6. Boil innards in clear water for 45 minutes. Remember to salt the water and add ginger, star anise, and various herbs such as thyme and rosemary!
  7. Place the blood noodles and innards into a chilled bowl. Pour a mixture of soy sauce, chili oil, and scallion oil over for taste!
