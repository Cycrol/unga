Human Leg Barbeque: OPEN FIRE VERSION

  1. Murder a fellow human, preferably one of a healthy age and muscular status.
  2. Harvest their quadriceps using a scalpel. REMEMBER TO NOT LEAVE ANY TENDONS.
  3. Salt the meat with flakey sea salt, paprika, garam masala, and some other strong herbs and spices.
  4. Throw onto a fire for 15 to 20 minutes, depending on temperature. BEST ENJOYED MEDIUM WELL! MEDIUM RARE DOES NOT WORK ON LEG MEAT!
